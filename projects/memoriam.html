Memoriam –
an interactive object
to overcome distances

Death seems to be one of the hardest topics to ever talk about. Even harder when a close person actually dies. In those times the best way to grief is by having the support of someone else. But what if our friends and family are hundreds and hundreds of miles away? Therefore we created Memoriam.

Memoriam is a website connected to two boxes inside each other. Every time someone is expressing condolences, the box lights up. Simultaneously, the inner box goes down and reveals a video of the person reminiscing about. The purpose is to have the possibility to show support without being physically present.
