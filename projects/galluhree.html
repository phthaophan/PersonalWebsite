galluhree –
a virtual gallery using
an eye tracker

Due to the COVID-19 pandemic quite a few things have changed, including cultural events. This is an approach on a digital art experience, bringing a gallery to everyone's home and putting a twist on the regular ones everyone knows.

While visiting the website and looking at historic artworks, the algorithm is working on a new piece of art. Behind the scenes, an eyetracker is collecting all the viewer’s eye movements which are then transformed into a unique artwork.

See for yourself and create your own artwork! www.galluhree.web.app
